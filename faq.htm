FAQ

What are binary options?

Binary options is a derivative financial instruments, the peculiarity of which is in a fixed-income and risk. A trader must determine whether the price of the underlying asset is above or below the current (it is called “the strike price”). You can receive up to 90% per trade. More precise information on the payout can be found in each option. If the option is unprofitable (i.e., your prediction was incorrect), you can lose 100% of the investment. The amount of profit per option does not depend on the number of points that change the value of the underlying asset.

What is an underlying asset?

The underlying asset means a financial instrument (Forex currency pair, stock market’s securities, indices and products of commodities market).

What is a “Down” Option?

If you think the price of an underlying asset will go down during the life of the option, you place a trade on "DOWN".

What is an “UP” option?

If you think the price of an underlying asset will go up during the life of the option, you place a trade on "UP".

What is the investment amount?

It is the amount of your investment in an option.

How to invest money in an option?

It is very simple. You choose an option, underlying asset, expiry time, type of option (up/down or any other depending on the type of option) and the amount of the investment. After that you just click the "Purchase" button.

What is the payment amount of a profitable (“In the Money”) options?

If you bought a profitable option, you can see the amount of payout next to the asset on our trading platform.

What would happen if the option was unprofitable (“Out of Money”)?

If your prediction is incorrect, the amount of investment in the binary option will be deducted from the account.

What is the meaning by expiration time and level?

The expiry time is the time at which your option will be closed. The expiry level is the price of the underlying asset at the time of expiry.

The levels we present in the trading boxes are those at which Keystockoptions broker is willing to sell you the options. Moreover, these levels will not always coincide with current market prices.

In this event, the amount of the investment is returned to the customer. You do not lose anything but gain nothing either.
